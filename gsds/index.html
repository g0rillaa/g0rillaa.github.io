Usage Guide - Microcontroller (for own car)

1. Place the Microcontroller on the vehicle and connect the gps x, gps y, compass, speed inputs.

2. Connect the existing throttle/brake and steering nodes to the microcontroller. These should come directly out of a seat. Then connect the outputs of throttle/brake and steering to where the seat was originally connected to.

3. Connect a monitor to the monitor in composite input, and to the video output. The monitor can be any size but ideally at least 1x2.
